  • 16. 7. 2019

    Super Mario Mixer

    Work in progress. You can make your own arrangements of the level editor music from Super Mario Maker in this mixing deck webapp! There are 7 mixer tracks for each combination of the 4 games and 6 themes.

    The webapp uses a fork of howler.js, favicon.js and Materialize. The playback, mixing and panning already works, but I don't have the loop points yet and I have to finish drawing the instrument icons. Also, I'm not sure where to host the music...
